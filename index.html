AdvanHR Detailed Documentation

Introduction

AdvanHR is a web-based HR management platform that leverages conversational and generative AI to automate routine tasks, generate deep insights, and proactively support decision‑making. By integrating a natural‑language Virtual Assistant, dual‑mode Analytics, predictive Forecasting, Intelligent Document Processing, and a self‑service Chatbot, AdvanHR streamlines workflows and elevates the employee experience—all within a secure, cloud‑native environment.

Key Features

Virtual Assistant

Leverage a virtual assistant to handle repetitive and complex tasks using natural language processing.

Document Intelligence

Analyze and interpret documents to extract targeted information or generate summaries based on user queries.

Forecasting Engine

Use predictive analytics to anticipate trends like employee turnover and performance.

Smart Analytics

Enable workforce insights through user-driven and AI-powered analytics tools.

Employee Chatbot

Provide a chatbot for instant access to organizational information such as roles, scales, company policies.

Resume Screening

Resume screening identifies the most suitable candidates based on the job requirements.

Job Interviewing

Job interviewing involves making AI driven calls to candidates for the initial technical assessment.

Technical Overview

Virtual Assistant

LangSQL-mini for quick NL-to-SQL, LangSQL for high-accuracy conversions, all via Azure Cognitive Services.

Document Intelligence

Chunks of ≤500 tokens are embedded (Embedding-3 Large) and indexed in Azure AI Search with metadata for fast retrieval and external reranking is applied for the efficient results.

Forecasting Engine

Multiple models are utilized for training purposes using approximately 600,000 records. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and feature engineering are applied to enhance the model’s efficiency and performance. After thorough evaluation, the best-performing model is selected and employed for prediction.

Employee Chatbot

Azure AI Studio assistant trained on policies to provide contextual, accurate employee support.

Smart Analytics

User-driven custom queries via LangSQL-mini and AI-driven automated trend detection for deeper insights.

Resume Screening

Resumes are submitted and subsequently parsed, followed by contextual analysis. Based on this analysis, candidates are ranked according to relevance and context, and the top candidates are then selected.

Job Interviewing

Automated call is made to the contact number provided, during which a bot conducts an interview by asking a predefined set of questions. The candidate’s responses are recorded, and the bot may ask follow-up questions based on the answers given. These recorded responses are then analyzed, and the candidate is ranked accordingly.
