Introduction

In today's fast-paced business environment, efficient human resource management is crucial for organizational success. AdvanHR, a Microsoft-registered startup, is an innovative digital solution designed to enhance HR operations through automation, advanced analytics, and intelligent processing. It streamlines HR tasks and supports decision-making via a Virtual Assistant, Analytics, Forecasting, Document Intelligence, and a Chatbot for self-service support.

Key Features

Virtual Assistant

Natural language queries converted to SQL via LangSQL-mini and LangSQL for fast, accurate responses, hosted securely on Azure.

Document Intelligence

Upload PDFs, DOCX, TXT. Content is chunked (≤500 tokens), embedded (Embedding-3 Large), and indexed in Azure AI Search for instant Q&A.

Forecasting

Analyzes 600k+ records with EDA-driven feature engineering to predict employee turnover and sales trends.

AI-Driven Analytics

Automatically detects trends from data and generates insights, assisting HR with proactive decision-making.

Chatbot Support

24/7 employee self-service built in Azure AI Studio, trained on policy documents for accurate assistance.

Resume Screening

Automates resume filtering and initial technical assessments to shortlist qualified candidates efficiently.

Technical Overview

Virtual Assistant

LangSQL-mini for quick, low-resource NL-to-SQL; LangSQL for high-accuracy conversions. Hosted via Azure Cognitive Services.

Document Intelligence

Chunks of ≤500 tokens are embedded (Embedding-3 Large) and indexed in Azure AI Search with id, content, embeddings fields.

Forecasting Module

600,000+ records processed. EDA for feature selection and engineering, training predictive models for turnover and sales.

Chatbot System

Azure AI Studio assistant trained on policy docs, providing contextual responses to employee inquiries.

Analytics Module

User-Driven: Custom queries via LangSQL-mini
AI-Driven: Automated trend detection and question generation for insights.

Resume Screening

Filters and shortlists candidates based on predefined criteria; conducts initial technical screening calls.
